Methodology · Making Sense framework
How this campaign is built
The campaign follows the EU Making Sense participatory methodology (Fab Lab Barcelona / IAAC, 2015–2017), adapted by Fab Lab Bali for the Indonesian context. We're currently in Phase 1.
1 · ScopingCollect matters of concern from the community before deciding what to measure. Now.
2 · CommunityConvene local contributors — schools, banjar, NGOs, fab labs.
3 · PlanningCo-design what to measure, where, how often, in which language.
4 · SensingDeploy sensors and the citizen-reporting bot.
5+ · Awareness · Action · ReflectionRead data together, decide responses, document for the next bioregion.
