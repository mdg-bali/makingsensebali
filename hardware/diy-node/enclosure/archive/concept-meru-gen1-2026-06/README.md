# Archived: meru concept gen1 (M1–M3) — June 2026

First pass at the batik-fractal meru direction (ICD DEC-01 v1.1). Rejected
in self-review before reaching Tomas: tapering from the ground reads as a
Christmas tree, droopy eaves read as ruffles, and the pattern bands
disappeared under the overhangs.

Lesson carried into gen2: a meru is a BODY wearing a crown of crisp,
broad roofs — tiers diminish slowly, eave lines stay sharp, bands stay
visible. Gen2/gen3 live in `tools/concepts_meru.py`; Tomas approved
**N1 lima (gen3)** as the Phase-1 base form (ICD DEC-09, v1.2).
